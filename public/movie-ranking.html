The Best Movies According to Kian Zhong

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Secret Life of Walter Mitty

It is a feel-good movie about a middle-aged man trying to break out of his comfort zone to find a new meaning to life. I watched it during a time of immense pain and it provided me with comfort.

Iron Man

Superhero action movie that started the entire MCU and Robert Downey Jr. What more can I say?

John Wick

A surprisingly entertaining action film filled with gore and violence. Recommended for father-son bonding time.
